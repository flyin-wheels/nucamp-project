Commercial IOT

Our commercial team specializes in commercial properties large and small.

The M. Green Properties IOT Project.

M. Green properties needed to automate lighting in all 30 cabin rental properties they own. Cognition IOT made it seamless providing cloud based controls that do not require technologies that share private data.

Seattle Glass and Wood IOT Project

Our commercial team worked closely with SGW to find the right fit without costly changes to the 75 year old landmark. Ait quality and humidity monitoring, and lighting management helps to showcase the works of art known throughout the PNW.

MoPOP Virtual Headcount IOT Project

The Museum of Popular Culture in Seattle wanted a system to track the number of participants at the gallery for safety and comfort. Cognition IOT installed minimal video surveillance hardware with attendee privacy and anonymization capabilities.
